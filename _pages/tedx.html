About the Event

TED is a media organisation which posts talks online for free distribution under the slogan “ideas worth spreading”.

Every year the TEDxYouth club at Delhi Public School, R.K Puram hosts an independently organised TEDx event.
As the team's graphic designer, I was responsible for putting together the identity cards, banners, speaker profiles and posters.
